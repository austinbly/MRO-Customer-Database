Customer Database Overview and Goals

Goal: 
-Create database of customers and order history.
-Use to inform future decisions

Plan of Execution:
-use Github to keep revision control
-organize customer info into relevant format
-add in relevant info about order history (products ordered, order timing, etc.)
-visualize the data
-derive recommendations for action

Potential Outcomes:
-be able to predict customer order timing and size
-identify key customers
-encourage reorders by understanding customer habits
